Rules & Prizes

Everything you should know about ProgNova programming contest

Eligibility

There are two divisions for the contest:

• North America Division: Open to all students and alumni from North American universities.

• Open Divison: Everyone else

Rules

The ProgNova contest rules are based on the standard ICPC rules, with the following adjustments:

• Participation is individual.

• Contest duration is 3 hours.

• Access to local and online materials is allowed during the contest. However, contestants must not communicate or discuss the contest problems with any people during the contest. Sharing materials regarding contest problems is strictly prohibited.

Environment

• Contestants may use any single machine to compete.

• Solutions are submitted online to the Kattis judging platform. Supported languages are C/C++/Java/Python2/Python3. Please see here for the judging details on the supported languages.

Prize for North America Division

• 1st Place receives one $200 Amazon gift card.

• 2nd Place receives one $125 Amazon gift card.

• 3rd Place receives one $75 Amazon gift card.

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the contestants that solved more than 4 problems. Each winner is awarded a $50 amazon gift card.

• 5 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the contestants that solved more than 2 problems. Each winner is awarded a $30 amazon gift card.

• Each contestant can win at most 1 prize. If a contestant who solved more than 4 problems does not get the $50 award, he/she will be included in the raffle for $30 awards.

Prize for Open Division

• 2 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the contestants that solved more than 4 problems. Each winner is awarded a $30 amazon gift card.

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the contestants that solved more than 2 problems. Each winner is awarded a $20 amazon gift card.

• Each contestant can win at most 1 prize. If a contestant who solved more than 4 problems does not get the $30 award, he/she will be included in the raffle for $20 awards.

• Note that the amazon gift cards are in USD, and can only be used in US amazon store.
