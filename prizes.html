Prizes

Show off your programming skills and win prizes and swags!

The two divisions will compete together. Prizes will be awarded separately to the two divisions. Raffle prizes will be determined using this script.

ICPC Division

• 1st place: $100 Amazon gift card + Competitive Programming Book + Google Swags

• 2nd place: $60 Amazon gift card + Competitive Programming Book + Google Swags

• 3rd place: $30 Amazon gift card + Competitive Programming Book + Google Swags

• 10 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 20 finishers who are not in the top 3 in the ICPC Division. 5 winners will receive one $20 Amazon gift card. The other 5 winners will receive a Competitive Programming Book.

* Competitive Programming Book refers to Competitive Programming 3rd Edition by Steven Halim and Felix Halim.

Open Division

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 10 finishers in the Open Division. Each winner is awarded one $20 Amazon gift card.
